    Why FLNG?

    FLNG is an LNG processing plant
    and storage facility custom-fitted on
    a vessel with full capability to treat,
    process, store and offload LNG. It is
    a key solution by PETRONAS to
    monetise stranded gas fields in a
    safe economical and sustainable
    way. It includes a large number of
    deep-water gas reserves, especially
    in East Malaysia.

    Our facilities are smaller, nimbler,
    and have greater flexibility to better
    access small and stranded gas fields
    globally.
